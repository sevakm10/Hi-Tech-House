Is it possible to bring water, electricity and sewerage to the house?

Yes, of course, after the completion of the construction of the house, the connection to the water supply and communications is made. We carry out the entire range of works related to the construction of country houses.

Can you tell me what is included in the price of the plot?

The price includes: a land plot of 8-12 acres, access roads to it, a construction permit, a city- building plan, a plot layout plan, assignment of an address and all central communications (electricity, water supply, sewerage, gas).

Is it possible to get advice on building materials for my house?

Of course, it's possible. We will tell you about the advantages and disadvantages of certain materials, and advise you on what will best fit your ideas of a reliable, beautiful and comfortable home.
